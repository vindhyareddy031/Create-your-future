Objective of the Activity Done

Week 1
To collect information about the community.

Detailed Report

On day 1

We visited the school of our community service project to interact with the students during the 1st week.

On day 2

As we entered the school premises, we observed the infrastructure of the school which was very pleasant and serene.

On day 3

We met the students of the community to collect information about the school and the surrounding area.

On day 4

We verified each and every aspect of the school and analyzed the economic conditions affecting it.

On day 5

We continued the survey of the school, documenting plans of activities and researching the history of the school.
