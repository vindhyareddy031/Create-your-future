WEEKLY REPORT

Week-2 — Community Service Project

Mount Litera Group of High School

Objective of the Activity Done

Week-2 (7/7/25 to 12/7/25)
To collect the information about the community (Mount Litera Group of High School).

Detailed Report

On day 1

We visited the school for our community service project to interact with the students in the 2nd week.

On day 2

We went to the Principal and asked for permission to interview the school students.

On day 3

We communicated with the Principal and interviewed them to know about the history of the school.

On day 4

We interacted with school students to know about the school’s day-wise classes, programs, and games.

On day 5

Our team introduced ourselves to the school community and other people, explaining the community service project.

On day 6

Finally, we prepared a proper design based on the analysis of school members’ aspirations for their future careers.
